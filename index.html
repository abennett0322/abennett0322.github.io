About Me

I am a 26y/o guy currently living in San Antonio, Texas. I am married to my high school sweetheart, have a border collie named Whiskey, and recently became a proud father to a baby boy.

My passion for technology began early on when I took my first computer science class as a freshman in high school. After that I continued computer sicence for 2 more years until my senior year where I decided to focus on music instead. I was a percussionist in band, and at the time I thought that's what I wanted to do for the rest of my life. When I went off to college, I quickly gave up my future with music to pursue a future as an entrepenur. I enrolled in the College of Business and had my sights set on majoring in Marketing. After 3painful years of struggling through business my grades dropped drastically and I was kicked from school. It was at that lowest point of not knowing what to do anymore that I rediscovered my passion for programming. I enrolled in the College of Sciences, redescovered my passion for programming, and the rest is history.

I currently work remotely as a ServiceNow Developer/Consultant at Highmetric building custom applications for business and organizations.

I love to talk to new people, and hearing about new opportunities. If you think I may be able to bring something to your team... LET ME KNOW! I Look forward to hearing from you!
